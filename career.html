We, at SUN Mobility, are passionate about making our cities cleaner and greener

What do we do?

SUN Mobility is engaged in the business of designing, developing and operating energy infrastructure for electric mobility for a range of electric vehicles in the shared mobility sector. We are constantly developing innovative technology solutions in the areas of Lithium-ion batteries, battery-vehicle integration, battery charging and quick-interchange stations and an end-to-end platform for software management.

Electric. Shared. Connected

SUN Mobility started with a dream of changing the way people commute in the future. Now we are very well on our way to transforming urban public transportation in India.

If you are stirred by a constant desire to usher change in the world around you, to work for the larger good and make a lasting impact on the society, then we'd love to talk!
